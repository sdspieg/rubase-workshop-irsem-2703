🚀 RuBase vs. StratBase: Two Brands, One Platform

RuBase workshops focus on Russia, Eurasia, and regional studies with area-specific examples and case studies.
StratBase workshops emphasize broader strategic analysis, policy research, and global applications.

Both use the same cutting-edge AI methodologies - the difference is in framing and examples to best serve different research communities.
