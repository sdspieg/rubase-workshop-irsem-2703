## Research Implications

### For Ottoman Banking Research:
1. **Use multiple search strategies**: Don't rely solely on institutional names
2. **Consider geographical terms**: "Galata", "Pera", "Constantinople" may surface different results
3. **Language matters**: Turkish-language papers may not be fully indexed for English searches

### For the Workshop:
This is a perfect example of why we teach:
- **Multiple search approaches**: Same topic, different angles
- **Critical evaluation**: Understanding what you're NOT finding
- **The importance of domain knowledge**: Knowing that Galata = Ottoman banking district
