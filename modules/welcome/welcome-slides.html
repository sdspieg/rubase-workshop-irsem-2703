📖 Case Study: Ottoman Bank Research

Real-world application of workshop methods

We'll walk through a complete research project from initial query to final analysis, demonstrating the power of multiple search strategies and systematic validation.

🔍 Discovery Phase
  • "Ottoman Bank" → 511 papers
  • "Galata district" AND bankers → 6 papers
  • Galata AND banking → 306 papers
  • Critical finding: Language indexing gaps
📊 Analysis Results
  • 99.8% open access rate
  • 167-year temporal span (1859-2026)
  • Cross-linguistic research patterns
  • Geographic distribution insights
🤖 LLM Processing
  • Automated relevance assessment
  • Thematic categorization
  • Quality validation workflows
  • Bias detection and correction

This demonstrates exactly what you'll be able to do with your own research topics.
