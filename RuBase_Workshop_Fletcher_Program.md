# RuBase Methods Workshop: AI-Augmented Research

**Location:** The Fletcher School
**Dates:** March 11–13, 2026
**Time:** 5:30 PM – 8:30 PM each evening

## Workshop Overview

This three-day workshop introduces participants to AI-augmented research methods using the RuBase/StratBase approach. Participants will learn to leverage bibliometric tools, build custom taxonomies, and use large language models for systematic research analysis.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this workshop, you will have:
- A corpus specification for your research topic
- A custom taxonomy tailored to your research needs
- Sample LLM classifications of your data
- Visualizations of your research findings
- A methodology section ready for your research paper

## Workshop Materials

The following presentation materials support each session:
- Part 1: Setting the Scene
- Part 2: Building Corpora
- Part 3: Building Taxonomies
- Part 4: Using LLMs to Annotate Corpora with Taxonomies
